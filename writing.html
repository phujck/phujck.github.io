Prize-Winning Work

🏆 2nd Place — FQxI Essay Competition 2025–26

Spanspermia: Does Life Come from Outer Hilbert Space?

Foundational Questions Institute (FQxI) · Theme: "How Quantum is Life?"

An argument from first principles against the notion that physiological-scale quantum phenomena play a meaningful role in biology. Drawing on the algebraic structure of quantum theory, the essay contends that life is better understood through classical stochastic models than through appeals to quantum coherence — and that the question itself reveals more about our assumptions than about biology.

Quantum Frontiers (Caltech) · Quantum-Steampunk Short-Story Contest, 2023

A short story blending quantum thermodynamics with steampunk fiction — because the second law of thermodynamics is more fun when there are top hats involved. Selected as first runner-up from a competitive international field.
