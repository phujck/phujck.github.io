Gerard McCaul

Research Associate in Quantum Reservoir Computing
Loughborough University, Department of Physics

I'm a theoretical physicist working at the intersection of quantum dynamics, control theory, and machine learning. My research asks how we can exploit the physics of open quantum systems — from engineering material responses with tailored laser pulses, to building the next generation of quantum and photonic computing architectures. When not doing physics, I am suffering from musical and authorial delusions.
