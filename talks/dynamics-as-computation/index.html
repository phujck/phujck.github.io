Dynamics as Computation

A visual talk on when physical dynamics begins to look like computation.

Talk

Overview

The talk opens on the title question, then moves into a concrete neuromorphic motivation before building through the tension between smooth dynamical evolution and discrete computation, reservoir dynamics, Koopman and KvN operator pictures, quantum signatures such as phase and quasiprobability, and finally optimisation problems encoded into Hamiltonian ground states.
