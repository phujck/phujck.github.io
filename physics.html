Research

What I work on

🎛️

Quantum Control

Designing laser fields and control protocols to steer quantum systems toward desired behaviours — from mimicking materials to suppressing optical responses.

🌊

Open Quantum Systems

Exact stochastic methods and influence functionals for systems interacting with complex environments. My PhD focus and an enduring interest.

🧠

Reservoir Computing

Quantum and photonic reservoir architectures — minimal designs with maximal expressivity, exploring where quantum advantage actually resides.

〰️

Superoscillations

Band-limited waves oscillating faster than their fastest component — applied to spectroscopy, sensing, and Bose-Einstein condensate confinement.

🔄

Koopman Methods

Hilbert-space representations of classical mechanics — unitary transformations, wave operators, and the deep parallels between classical and quantum dynamics.

⚡

Non-Linear Optics

Non-uniqueness of driving fields, optical indistinguishability, and the engineering of optical responses in strongly interacting systems.
